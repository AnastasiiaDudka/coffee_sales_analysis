# Key Business Insights

## 🇺🇸 Best Market

**Finding**

The United States generated the highest revenue, had the largest customer base, and the highest number of orders.

**Recommendation**

Focus future marketing campaigns and advertising budget on the US market.

---

##  Product Performance

**Finding**

Exc coffee generated the highest revenue, highest average order value, and sold the largest number of units.

**Recommendation**

Prioritize Exc coffee in promotions and maintain sufficient inventory.

---

##  Roast Performance

**Finding**

Light roast achieved the highest revenue and average order value.

**Recommendation**

Promote Light roast products as premium offerings.

---

##  Package Size

**Finding**

The 2.5 kg package generated the highest revenue despite having fewer orders than smaller packages.

**Recommendation**

Maintain strong inventory levels for larger package sizes.

---

##  Loyalty Program

**Finding**

Customers without a loyalty card generated higher total revenue and higher average order value.

**Recommendation**

Review the current loyalty program and redesign customer incentives.

---

##  Revenue Concentration

**Finding**

The Top 5 products generated approximately 27% of total company revenue.

**Recommendation**

The company has a well-diversified product portfolio and is not overly dependent on a single product.
